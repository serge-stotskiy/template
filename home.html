About LNR Portal

We help our users perform quick, detailed analysis by phone number. Moreover, for our loyal customers we have implemented the ability to conduct analysis thanks to a pre-filled exel file, which corresponds to a certain list of requirements. Thanks to this analysis users have the ability to analyze an unlimited number of numbers simultaneously on selected parameters and indicators.

If you occasionally need to analyze just a few numbers, we recommend that you create a basic account and replenish the account as needed.

If you're interested in systematic and extensive phone number analysis, then look into more advanced plans, where you can analyze many positions at once and enjoy all the benefits of saving with our service.
